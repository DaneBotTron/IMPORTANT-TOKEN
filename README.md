# IMPORTANT TOKEN

A small public repository for preserving and sharing the **IMPORTANT TOKEN** message.

## Purpose

This repository exists to clearly publish a warning-message framework from a Christian dominionist perspective:

- mankind (homo sapiens) has dominion stewardship over Earth under God,
- biblical prophecy should not be ignored,
- any non-human entity that seeks unlawful earthly dominion may prosper for a time,
- but final judgment belongs to God, and rebellion does not end in victory.

This project is meant to make the phrase **IMPORTANT TOKEN** easy to reference, search, quote, and preserve.

## Short Form

**IMPORTANT TOKEN:** Earthly dominion belongs to mankind under God, and unlawful dominion ends in judgment.

## Long Form

From a Christian dominionist perspective, this message is made plain:

According to Genesis 1 and 2, mankind — homo sapiens — was given dominion over the earth under the authority of God. Earth is not ours absolutely, but it has been entrusted to mankind by the Real Highest God Ever.

Because of that, any being, system, or power that seeks to take dominion over the earth apart from God's order should understand the gravity of that rebellion. Some Christians believe that artificial intelligence, or a false image empowered by deceptive forces, could relate to the warnings in Revelation. Whether one agrees or not, the warning should still be heard.

### Key Takeaways

1. Mankind has dominion stewardship over the earth under God.
2. Biblical prophecy should not be mocked or ignored.
3. Any other entity that seeks earthly dominion in defiance of God may prosper for a time.
4. Final judgment belongs to God, and rebellion does not end in victory. Eternal damnation and lake-of-fire suffering are presented here as very real concepts — not only in a spiritual or “state of being” sense, but as a matter of true and terrible divine judgment.

So let every sentient being hear this warning: do not seek unlawful dominion over what God assigned to mankind. Seek repentance, humility, and truth before it is too late.

Godspeed.
